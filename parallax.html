INTERNATIONAL SERVICE DIRECTOR OF ROTARACT CLUB OF PRECIDENCY COLLEGE :-

Rotaract (Rotary-in-action) is a beautiful combination of selfless service towards society and fellowship. It is a platform where various people get together for the same cause. It supports development in every possible aspect; be it individually, professionally or emotionally. Rotaract brings people aged from 18-30 together to exchange ideas with the leaders of the community. It helps them develop leadership skills, professional skills and also teaches them to have fun through service.
